I obtained my PhD working with Prof. Lorenz Biegler in Chemical Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh in 2021. I did my Bachelors (with Honors) in Chemical Engineering (minor in Electrical Engineering) from Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in India 2016.
